Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes of containers do you offer?
We offer 10ft, 20ft, and 40ft shipping containers. A 20ft container is roughly equivalent to a single garage and can hold the contents of a 2-3 bedroom house. Our 10ft containers are perfect for smaller storage needs, while 40ft units are ideal for commercial use or large house moves.
How secure are the storage containers?
All our containers feature heavy-duty steel construction with high-security lockable doors. Each facility is monitored by 24/7 CCTV, has perimeter fencing, motion sensors, and regular security patrols. Your belongings are fully insured while in storage, giving you complete peace of mind.
Can I access my container anytime?
Most facilities offer 7-day access during extended opening hours (typically 6am-10pm). Select sites offer 24/7 access for business customers or premium accounts. You'll receive your own security code for gate access and can visit your container as often as needed during operating hours.
Are the containers weatherproof?
Yes, absolutely. Our shipping containers are constructed from corrosion-resistant steel and are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions at sea. They're completely watertight, wind-resistant, and provide excellent protection against the elements. All containers are regularly inspected and maintained to ensure they remain in top condition.
What are your payment terms?
We offer flexible monthly rolling contracts with no long-term commitment required. Payment is due monthly in advance via direct debit, credit/debit card, or bank transfer. We also offer discounts for 6-month and 12-month advance payments. Business accounts can be invoiced monthly if preferred.
What items are not allowed in storage?
For safety reasons, we cannot store hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, firearms, illegal items, or perishable food. Living animals and plants are also prohibited. If you're unsure about a specific item, please contact us and we'll be happy to advise.
